Jumping stilts

Alexander Böck, the inventor of jumping stilts, studied aerospace technology at the University Munich Engineering and obtained a Master's Degree in Engineering. Initially a turbojet engineer, he explored human propulsion methods by studying Tendons of Achilles in animals such as kangaroos. He developed a prototype designed as a bionic extension of the calf. This device To a human to jump like a kangaroo.

pneumatic stilts

The only acrobatics company on pneumatic stilts in the world

The Tac O Tac Company was created by two multidisciplinary artists:
Karim RANDE (Propane) and Brice DURAND (Butane)

Stilts for many years, it is on pneumatic stilts that they stand out.
By mixing dance, acrobatics and aerial acrobats they offer us unheard of. Based on balance, strength and poetry, this explosive and gentle universe leaves no one indifferent.

In 2010 they begin with a first duo for Fournales, world leader in the manufacture of pneumatic stilts.

In 2011 they created the first number mixing hands to hands and acrobatics on pneumatic stilts. A vertiginous feat they are the only ones to propose.
Then in the same year they integrate projects of scale:
They will start by playing in Snow White with Julia Roberts. On this project they will teach the stilts to famous stuntmen, integrate acrobatics in battle on stilts and realize these prowess in front of the camera for the pleasure of the spectators.
From shooting they will go directly to Belgium for one of the biggest gatherings of stilts. Waders from all over the world will discover through them the modernity of stilts and their aerial possibilities.
In 2012 they leave their very first creation:

Special Conquest
A spectacular story about the story of three Soviet adventurers trying to get to the moon.
